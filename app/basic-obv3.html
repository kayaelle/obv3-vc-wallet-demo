Demo Open Badge 3.0

    But first, just provide a page with a link

  1. Download & install the Learner Credential Wallet -- add link
  2. From wallet, click "add credential"
  3. Scan QR code
  4. Wallet will show a demo log in screen. This mocks how you may authenticate with an issuer from the wallet - click log in to continue.
  5. Once the issuer confirms yor log in, the wallet sends it the decentralized idenitifier that was created when you installed the wallet.
  6. The issuer creates and digitally signs the badge and sends it back to the wallet.
  7. From the wallet you can review and accept the badge.
